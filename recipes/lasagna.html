Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb. ground beef
  • 1 lb. breakfast sausage
  • 2 cloves, minced garlic
  • 2 cans, whole tomatoes
  • 2 cans, tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp. dried parsley
  • 2 tbsp. dried basil
  • improvise the rest!

Instructions

  1. Bring large pot of water to a boil
  2. Cook ground beef and sausage with garlic in a saucepan
  3. Mix cheese, eggs, parmesan, and salt
  4. Arrange noodles and add sauce/meat
