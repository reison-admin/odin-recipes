Sushi & Rice Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lb. fresh salmon
  • 2x 8 oz bags of brown rice (short-grain sticky rice is best)
  • 1 tbsp. dried seaweed
  • 1 tbsp. japanese rice seasoning mix
  • 1 tsp. gochujang salt mix

Instructions

  1. Sear the outside of salmon with oil in a pan
  2. Microwave rice bags
  3. Mix salmon, rice, and spices
  4. Eat it!
